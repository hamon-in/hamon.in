Case Study: Vyoma Media

Offerings: AI/ML

Company Profile

  • Company Proﬁle The leader in Indian digital signage space
  • Four million eyeballs daily across Indian Railway stations, bus stations etc.
  • 504 clients, 2512 campaigns, 17 states, 400 locations, 2121 displays 100+ Employees

Problem

Vyoma was looking to expand from 2000 to 5000 screens nationally. However their deployment time was more than 1 month. Updates required manual workforce for deployment and monitoring and testing had to be done onsite leading to high defect rate. Often these displays were in areas with poor intener connectivity

Solution

We built a device management system to manage and monitor the displays remotely on a centralized custom made dashboard. The ad-engine was rearchitected to reduce defects and increase reliability. Large ad videos required efficient sync and maintenance and at places with poor internet connectivity, Offline fallback was provided

Result

60% manual labour reduction for deployment and monitoring Deployment times were reduced from over a month to a week per city. Over 80% of defects were caught in the lab before deployment.

Technologies

Python, Shell, MQTT, AWS, Linux, various A/V tools
